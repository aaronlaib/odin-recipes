Stuffed Acorn Squash

Description:

A delicous recipe of acorn squash filled with sausage and various veggies. It is a filling and easy recipe to make. Cooks well in the oven.

Fill a pan with several of them to have leftovers!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground turkey or lose pork sausage
  • 3 small/med acorn squash
  • 1 small onion diced
  • 2 celery stalks diced
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 small apple diced
  • 1 Tbsp fresh rosemary leaves
  • 1 Tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tsp coconut oil
  • sea salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°. Cut squash lengthwise; scoop out seeds/strings. Brush squash halves with oil and sprinkle lightly with salt. Place squash face down on a lightly greased baking sheet, and roast 25-30 minutes, flip after 15 minutes. Squash should be slightly soft to the touch.
  2. While squash roasts, prepare stuffing. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add 1 Tbsp oil. Add meat, browning evenly. Remove from heat and transfer to a plate.
  3. Cook onions (about 3 min). Stir in garlic and cook for 1 min. Add apples, herbs, spinach, and salt/pepper. Cook for 2-3 min until apples are soft and spinach is wilted.
  4. Return sausage back to the skillet (and optional cranberries, pecans), turn heat to medium/low and continue to cook 2 minutes to blend flavors.
  5. Arrange squash halves face-up on baking sheet. Scoop stuffing mixture into each. Return pan to oven (optional: broiler) and heat until tops of stuffing and squash are browned, (about 5 min). Garnish with fresh minced parsley and serve./li>
