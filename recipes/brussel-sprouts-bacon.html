Brussel Sprouts and Bacon

Description:

A recipe I've made too many times to count. Great when made on stovetop or in oven. Best mass produced in oven.

Goes really well with rice and our very own vegan ranch. Best with two large pans.

Ingredients:

  • Brussel Sprouts - 4 pounds
  • Bacon - 24 ounces
  • Onion - 3 small
  • Apples - 2 or 3
  • Cooking oil - 2 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 400F
  2. Cook bacon in oven for 5-10 minutes
  3. Remove Bacon and set on plate
  4. Place other ingredients on pans
  5. Place bacon on top
  6. Cook for 30-40 minutes, switch pans halfway through.
