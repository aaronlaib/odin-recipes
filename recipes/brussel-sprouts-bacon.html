Brussel Sprouts and Bacon

Description:

A recipe I've made too many times to count. Great when made on stovetop or in oven. Best mass produced in oven.

Goes really well with rice and our very own vegan ranch.

Ingredients:

  • Brussel Sprouts - 4 pounds
  • Bacon - 24 ounces
  • Onion - 3 small
  • Apples - 2 or 3
  • Cooking oil - 2 tbsp
