Vegan Ranch Dressing

Description

This is a Dairy-Free, Paleo, Whole30 Ranch that's easy to make and perfect for veggie dipping, drizzling on a bed of greens, or as a dipper for all of your favorite dip-needing foods.

Our quick recipe makes it easy for you to say goodbye to highly-processed ranch dressings made with unpronounceable ingredients and cheap, inflammatory oils. Once you make it, you'll be saying hello creamy, dreamy, homemade ranch made with fresh, real-food ingredients.

Recipe adopted from therealfoodietitians.com

Ingredients:

  • Avocado Mayo (Primal Kitchen) - 1 cup
  • full-fat coconut milk - 1/2 cup
  • dried dill - 1 tsp
  • garlic powder - 1 tsp
  • onion powder - 1 tsp
  • sea salt - 1/2 tsp
  • ground black pepper - 1/2 tsp
  • Optional
    • dried chives - 1 tsp
    • parsley, preferably fresh - 2 tsp

Steps:

  1. Place all ingredients in a bowl (or food processor for best results) and mix until smooth and creamy.
  2. Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  3. Store in a covered container or mason jar in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
